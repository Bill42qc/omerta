Godhi Bank​

The Godhi Family serves as the financial backbone of Empire City, ensuring stability through the Empire Bank and $EMPIRE, the city's primary currency. They actively shape fiscal policies, underwrite Special Community Bonds, and maintain strict transparency with a public ledger tracking all $EMPIRE minting. All bank wallets remain publicly accessible, with 90% of the treasury secured in a multi-signature system and 10% kept in a designated wallet. This structure guarantees both security and flexibility, fostering a prosperous and resilient financial empire.

TAKING CONTROL OF FAMILY

Each Don can found their own Family (Sub DAO) in Empire City, using its utilities and city servants (bots). Submit a proposal to the Council of Dons, and if approved, it goes to the DAO for a vote. If accepted, you may found a new Family.

Newly founded Families receive full dev support from Mike $Empire for launching a collection with Omerta Art, with the mint minimum set to the floor price of SOLdiers.

The founding Don keeps 50% of mint funds, 25% goes to other staked Dons, 12.5% to the community treasury, and 12.5% to the Godfather for support.

Founding a Family grants automatic support from the Grand Mafia and its moderators, offering extensive services, utilities, Dapps, and bots for all NFT collections in Empire City.
